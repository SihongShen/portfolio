## Collaborator: [Yunchu (Helena) Qin](https://cloudie.work)  
This work is a surrealist visual experiment rooted in an existential crisis. By deconstructing the track "***Wie wird man seinen Schatten los?***" (***How can one escape one's own shadow?***) from the German musical ***Mozart!***, we have materialized the futile pursuit of life's meaning into a metaphorical nightmare.

The narrative orbits a singular, haunting interrogation: **"How can you die to live?"** It maps the creator's profound philosophical transition—from the acceptance of inherent void to the courageous act of self-defined purpose. Through hand-drawn animations that symbolize internal fractures, we stitched fragmented metaphors into a seamless, lucid dream. More than a private record of struggle, this film serves as a manifesto, urging the audience to forge their own coordinates of existence amidst a surreal reality.
